ABOUT ME

I came to Central Indiana, via Northeast Ohio in 2013, to attend Ball State University for my Masters degree in Music. I studied classical voice for 15+ years, before discovering that I had natural strength in analytical areas as well as artistic. I am a puzzle addict and a spreadsheet junkie, but I have a serious side as well!

I enrolled at Eleven Fifty Academy because I would like a career that energizes me, where I never run out of new things to learn and master. I believe that creativity and imagination are not only useful for art, but can be applied in many more “mundane” ways, such as: looking at issues from every angle, generating innovative ideas, or even putting yourself in another person’s shoes.

I live with my partner, Aaron and our precious cat, Crowley. I'm getting really into the WFH lifestye. In my spare time, I am an avid home-cooking gourmet, I enjoy writing both fiction and non-fiction, and I adore reading fantasy novels. Pre-quarantine, I could also be found creating bizarre characters to play in Dungeons & Dragons games. In a past life (aka my mid-twenties), I earned my black belt in Taekwondo, and if I ever have free time again, I’d like to resume my training.
